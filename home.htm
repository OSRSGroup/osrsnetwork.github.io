OSRS Open. Bring your own code or use ours.

OSRS Open is a non-profit, open source software organization with the primary goal of providing advanced computing capabilities principally for the analytics, big data, cybersecurity, defense, engineering and scientific communities.

OSRS Group. Capturing the horizon.

OSRS Group is a collaborative research and development consultancy, with the primary goal of researching, developing and providing advanced computing capabilities. We achieve this goal through fostering collaboration between our member partners.

Pacific Northwest National Lab (PNNL). Discovery in action.

Discovery in action describes what we do at PNNL, which has been operated by Battelle and its predecessors since our inception in 1965. For more than 50 years, we've advanced the frontiers of science and engineering in the service of our nation and the world. We make fundamental scientific discoveries that illuminate the mysteries of our planet and the universe. We apply our scientific expertise to tackle some of the most challenging problems in energy, the environment, and national security.

US Army Corps of Engineers (USACE). Build Strong.

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers has approximately 37,000 dedicated Civilians and Soldiers delivering engineering services to customers in more than 130 countries worldwide. With environmental sustainability as a guiding principle, our disciplined Corps team is working diligently to strengthen our Nation’s security by building and maintaining America’s infrastructure and providing military facilities where our servicemembers train, work and live. We are also researching and developing technology for our war fighters while protecting America’s interests abroad by using our engineering expertise to promote stability and improve quality of life. We are energizing the economy by dredging America’s waterways to support the movement of critical commodities and providing recreation opportunities at our campgrounds, lakes and marinas. And by devising hurricane and storm damage reduction infrastructure, we are reducing risks from disasters. Our men and women are protecting and restoring the Nation’s environment including critical efforts in the Everglades, the Louisiana coast, and along many of our Nation’s major waterways. The Corps is also cleaning sites contaminated with hazardous, toxic or radioactive waste and material in an effort to sustain the environment. Through deeds, not words, we are BUILDING STRONG.

US National Park Service (US NPS). Discover America's Story: The National Parks.

Since 1916, the National Park Service has been entrusted with the care of our national parks. With the help of volunteers and partners, we safeguard these special places and share their stories with more than 275 million visitors every year. But our work doesn't stop there.
